Forex is the world’s largest financial market with a daily trading volume of over $6 trillion. Forex is perfect for traders who want to take advantage of currency fluctuations as it is open 24 hours a day, 5 days a week.

Major pairs (majors): EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY and others. These pairs feature high liquidity and tight spreads

Exotic pairs: Combinations of rare currencies such as USD/TRY or EUR/SEK. Offer high volatility and potentially large profits

Indices

Indices are baskets of stocks of leading companies that reflect the health of an industry or the market as a whole. Index trading allows market players to diversify risk and profit from the general direction of the market without having to focus on individual stocks

Buy indices

Trade indices of world countries:

American indices: S&P 500, Dow Jones, NASDAQ. These indices reflect the dynamics of the largest US companies

European indices: DAX (Germany), FTSE 100 (UK), CAC 40 (France)

Asian indices: Nikkei 225 (Japan), Hang Seng (Hong Kong), CSI 300 (China)

Commodity assets

Commodities are traditional safe-haven investments that allow traders to profit from price fluctuations. With DigitalSpectro, you can invest in both precious metals and energy resources, with multiple opportunities for short-term and long-term trading strategies

Buy commodities

A wide range of commodities:

Precious metals: gold (XAU/USD), silver (XAG/USD), platinum. Gold is especially popular as a risk hedging instrument

Energy resources: oil (WTI, Brent), natural gas. These assets are sensitive to geopolitical events and macroeconomic data

Agricultural commodities: coffee, sugar, wheat. An excellent choice for traders interested in seasonal trends

Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies offer unique opportunities for traders due to the high volatility and innovative nature of the market. With DigitalSpectro, you can trade the most popular digital assets, allowing you to profit from both short-term and long-term market movements

Popular cryptocurrencies to trade:
