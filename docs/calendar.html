How to use the Economic Calendar

Plan your trading decisions with a reliable partner

Step 1

Get familiar with the interface

On the main page of the Economic Calendar, you will see a list of upcoming and past events, the dates and times they were published, and the importance of the event (low, medium or high).

Step 2

Choose the event of interest

Click on any event to get detailed information including descriptions, forecasts and previous results. This will help you better understand the impact the event may have on the market.

Step 3

Analyze the data

Compare the projected values with previous results to identify possible deviations. Evaluate how the market has reacted to similar events in the past and use this information to make decisions.

Step 4

Integrate into your strategy

Use information from the Economic Calendar to adapt your trading strategy. For example, if an important economic event is expected, you may decide to pause trading or modify your positions.

Step 5

Stay up to date with the latest news

Stay up-to-date with news and analytics related to calendar events. This will help you better understand the context and potential impact on markets.

Step 6

Perform post-analysis

After events are released, go back to the calendar to study how they affected the markets. This gives you the opportunity to learn from the past experience and improve your forecasts in the future.
